About Us

SnapSaver is an application designed for students or people who are living on a budget. This app helps with looking at specific product and comparing prices at different grocery stores to provide you the lowest price. With crowdsourced data from different customer, we can help each other at finding which store has the lowest prices or ongoing promotions.
